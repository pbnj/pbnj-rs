About

Peter Benjamin is a Software Engineer and a Developer Advocate, focusing on infrastructure, Kubernetes, Security, and DevOps at Teradata. Formerly, Peter was a Security Engineer at Symantec and Intuit.

Peter has a deep technical background in the Go programming language, Kubernetes, and Docker. Peter is involved in the community as a Kubernetes contributor, blogger, and a co-organizer for the San Diego Kubernetes and the San Diego Golang meet-ups. Peter lives in San Diego and spends his free time with his family hiking or going to the beach.
